Quinoa Salad

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup uncooked quinoa
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 medium cucumber
  • 1 medium red bell pepper (diced)
  • ¾ cup red onion (finely chopped)
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • ¼ cup lemon juice
  • ½ teaspoon fine sea salt

Instructions:

  1. Rinse quinoa under cold water.
  2. Combine quinoa and water in a medium saucepan. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to a gentle simmer.
  3. Cook uncovered until water is absorbed (about 15 minutes).
  4. Remove from heat, cover, and let rest for 5 minutes. Fluff with a fork and cool.
  5. In a large bowl combine cucumber, bell pepper, onion.
  6. In a small bowl, whisk together garlic and salt until blended.
  7. Add cooled quinoa to the veggie bowl.
  8. Season with black pepper and adjust salt if needed.
  9. Let salad sit for 5–10 minutes before serving for best flavor.
  10. Keeps well in the fridge for up to 4 days. Serve chilled or at room temperature.
